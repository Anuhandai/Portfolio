    Problem The request was for a mobile app that allows users to use cloud storage app or organizing app depending on the user's needs and market entry. Seeing potential in the cloud technology space, the client wanted a way to add the social aspect of sharing with friends as well. My task is to find out the user’s unique needs and wants through user research and discovery. Additional requests were that this app allows users to: • Save content you find on the web (links, images, videos, etc.) • Organize content using categories, tags, groups, and/or folders • Create content • Upload files (videos, images, PDFs, etc.) from a computer or mobile device solutions The Wale app is cloud storage for anybody who wants to see their wardrobe visually. It allows you to easily create outfits and get your friends’ opinions on your purchase or outfit choice. Best of all, everything is in the cloud. Because people naturally forget things, Wale makes it easy for users to see their wardrobe at any time and gain inspiration from friends. Not only are users encouraged to make more conscious choices about their next purchase, but they can also get the most out of their more organized wardrobes.
